Your Journey to Becoming a Budget Boss!

Master financial literacy, budgeting, saving, investing, and smart money decisions — step by step.

Your Learning Journey

Module 1 – Concept, Value and Types of Money

  • Understand the origin, evolution, and value of money — from ancient barter to digital payments and cryptocurrencies.
  • Basic Concepts about Money
  • Types of Money
  • Cryptocurrency
  • Quiz – Module 1

You’ll Learn: What money is and why it’s valuable. The types of money — commodity, fiat, digital, and fiduciary. How cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and Ethereum work. The role of money as a medium of exchange, store of value, and unit of account.

Goal: Build a strong foundation of how money evolved, how it works, and why it’s important in our daily lives.

Module 2 – Basics of Budgeting, Saving, and Investment

  • Learn how to plan, save, and invest like a pro — making smart money choices for real-life situations.
  • Basics of Budgeting
  • Basics of Saving
  • Basics of Investment
  • Profit & Loss
  • Quiz – Module 2
  • Activity: Plan your dream birthday party under ₹5,000 — balancing fun and finance!

You’ll Learn: How to plan budgets for needs, wants, and savings goals. The importance of saving and how interest helps your money grow. How to invest in stocks, bonds, mutual funds, or skills that build value. Understanding profit and loss — how income, expenses, and returns shape financial outcomes.

Goal: Develop practical money skills — budgeting, saving, investing, and tracking performance.

Module 3 – Final Capstone Project

Put everything you’ve learned into action using an interactive simulator that shows how your money grows over time.

Compound Interest & Investment Simulator

You’ll Learn:

  • How compound interest multiplies your savings.
  • How different investments perform over time (stocks, gold, mutual funds, etc.).
  • How to adjust risk levels and rebalance portfolios.

Reflective Questions:

  • Which investment grows fastest and why?
  • How does time affect growth?
  • How would you balance risk and reward?

Goal: Apply your learning in a real-world simulation and become a true “Budget Boss.”

You’ll Learn To

  • Understand the value and evolution of money
  • Manage and plan budgets efficiently
  • Build strong saving and investing habits
  • Calculate profit, loss, and returns confidently
  • Make smart financial decisions using real-world data

Why This Matters

This course helps students build essential life skills in financial literacy — empowering them to think critically, manage money wisely, and plan for the future.
